The Cloud

I wandered through the sky so blue,
Where clouds drift by and dreams come true.
A wispy white, so soft and light,
Embraced the day and kissed the night.

The sun plays hide and seeks in vain,
As I lay watching through the rain.
With every shape that drifts above,
I find a piece of endless love.

So when you find yourself alone,
Just look above and see the stone.
For every cloud and every gleam,
Holds a fragment of a dream.
